FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior coding experience for Manual Testing?
Absolutely not! Manual Testing is perfect for beginners. No coding knowledge is required. We start from scratch and cover everything from software basics to professional test case writing. Many of our successful alumni came from non-IT backgrounds like commerce, arts, and science.
Is ISTQB certification included in the course fee?
ISTQB exam prep materials, mock tests, and coaching are included in the Manual Testing and Automation Testing programs. The ISTQB exam fee (approximately ₹7,500) is paid separately by the student directly to ISTQB. We guarantee you'll be 100% exam-ready.
What does "100% Placement Assistance" mean?
Our placement assistance includes: resume building and optimization, LinkedIn profile makeover, mock technical and HR interviews, portfolio review, and direct referrals to our 450+ hiring partners. We don't guarantee a specific salary but we guarantee our best efforts until you're placed. Our 2024 batch had a 96% placement rate within 90 days of course completion.
Can I get an EMI for the course fee?
Yes! We offer No Cost EMI (0% interest) through major banks and fintech partners like HDFC, ICICI, Axis, and Razorpay. Starting from ₹3,500/month for the Manual Testing course. You can also pay in 2–3 instalments directly to us with no extra charge. Contact our counselor for the exact EMI plan for your chosen program.
Are the classes recorded? What if I miss a session?
Yes, every live session is recorded and uploaded to our student portal within 4 hours. You get lifetime access to all recordings, notes, and materials. You can also attend any missed topic in the next batch free of cost. We make sure no student ever falls behind due to unavoidable absences.
Do I get a certificate after completing the course?
Yes! You receive a Tecch1World Course Completion Certificate recognized by our 450+ hiring partners. For the internship program, you also receive a Letter of Recommendation (LOR) and an internship experience certificate. These certificates are digitally verifiable and carry QR codes for employer verification.
Is the internship program open to students who haven't taken any course here?
The internship program is primarily designed for graduates of our courses, but we occasionally accept external candidates for specific projects if they have demonstrated skills through a short screening test. Priority is always given to our own students. Contact us to discuss your specific situation.
